**Reel Tail Motion Studio**

Reel Tail Motion Studio is a professional-grade, browser-based motion graphics engine designed to create high-conversion animated tails for social media Reels, TikToks, and Shorts.

Instead of static end-cards, this tool generates a high-definition 9:16 vertical video featuring your custom QR codes (Website and WhatsApp) with smooth, high-energy animations.

**Features**

Dynamic Motion Graphics: Smooth staggered entrance animations for QR cards, floating neon background orbs, and pulsing typography.

Custom QR Integration: Client-side image processing—upload your own QR codes and they are instantly mapped onto the 3-tilting motion cards.

Brand Customization: Full control over primary and accent colors to match your brand identity.

HD Video Export: Uses the MediaRecorder API to capture 60FPS canvas streams and export them as high-quality .webm files.

9:16 Optimized: Built specifically for vertical social media formats (1080x1920).

Privacy Focused: All processing happens locally in the browser. No images or videos are ever uploaded to a server.

**How It Works**

Upload Assets: Select your Website QR and WhatsApp QR images.

Contact Info: Enter your WhatsApp number to be featured in the footer animation.

Style: Adjust the color pickers to see real-time updates in the motion preview.

Export: Click "Export Animated Reel". The app records a perfect 5-second loop of the animation and downloads it to your device.

**Technical Stack**

HTML5 Canvas: Powers the high-performance animation loop.

Tailwind CSS: Modern, glassmorphism-inspired UI for the setup dashboard.

MediaRecorder API: Captures the canvas stream for high-bitrate video generation.

Plus Jakarta Sans: A modern, geometric font for high-end readability.

**Installation**

Since this is a client-side tool, no installation is required.

Clone the repository:

git clone [https://github.com/saheermk/reel-tail.git](https://github.com/saheermk/reel-tail.git)
